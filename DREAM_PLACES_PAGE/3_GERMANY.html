WHAT AND WHERE IS BRANDENBURG GATE?

          Brandenburg Gate is a neoclassical monument located in Berlin, Germany. It was built in the late 18th century as a symbol of peace and unity, and it has since become an iconic landmark of the city and a symbol of Germany's reunification.

WHY DO I WANT TO VISIT THIS PLACE?

          As a symbol of peace and unity, I am intrigued by the historical significance of the Brandenburg Gate in Berlin, Germany. I want to stand beneath this neoclassical monument and reflect on its role in Germany's past and present, and also explore the vibrant city of Berlin.

OTHER THINGS TO DO AROUND THE AREA:

1. Learn about Berlin's tumultuous history at the nearby Berlin Wall Memorial.

2. Explore the Reichstag Building, which houses the German parliament and offers panoramic views of the city from its dome.

3. Visit the Holocaust Memorial, a poignant tribute to the victims of the Nazi regime.

4.Take a stroll down Unter den Linden, a famous boulevard lined with historic buildings and museums.

5. Enjoy the lively atmosphere of Potsdamer Platz, a bustling square filled with restaurants and entertainment venues.
