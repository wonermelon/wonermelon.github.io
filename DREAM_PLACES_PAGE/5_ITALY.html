WHAT AND WHERE IS LEANING TOWER OF PISA?

          Leaning Tower of Pisa is a freestanding bell tower located in Pisa, Italy. It is known for its unintended tilt caused by a foundation that was too soft, and it has become a popular tourist attraction as visitors come to marvel at its unusual angle.

WHY DO I WANT TO VISIT THIS PLACE?

          The Leaning Tower of Pisa is an architectural marvel and a testament to human ingenuity. I want to visit this iconic Italian landmark to witness its unique tilt, take in its beauty, and learn about its history and significance to the Italian people.

OTHER THINGS TO DO AROUND THE AREA:

1. Take a cheesy photo pretending to hold up the tower.

2. Climb the tower's spiral staircase to the top for beautiful views of Pisa.

3. Explore the nearby Piazza dei Miracoli, a UNESCO World Heritage site that includes the tower, a cathedral, and a baptistery.

4.Visit the Camposanto Monumentale, a beautiful cemetery with stunning frescoes and sculptures.

5. Enjoy a slice of traditional Tuscan pizza at one of the many local restaurants.
