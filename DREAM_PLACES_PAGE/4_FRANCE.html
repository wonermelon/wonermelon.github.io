WHAT AND WHERE IS EIFFEL TOWER?

          Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower located in Paris, France. It was built in the late 19th century as a temporary structure for the World's Fair, but it has since become an enduring symbol of Paris and a major tourist attraction.

WHY DO I WANT TO VISIT THIS PLACE?

          The Eiffel Tower has always been a symbol of romance, beauty, and artistry. I want to experience the stunning views of Paris from the top, the thrill of ascending its heights, and soak in the atmosphere of one of the world's most iconic landmarks.

OTHER THINGS TO DO AROUND THE AREA:

1. Take the elevator to the top of the tower for stunning views of Paris.

2. Have a picnic on the Champs de Mars, a large public park that stretches out in front of the tower.

3. Explore the nearby Trocadéro Gardens, which offer more picturesque views of the tower.

4.Walk along the Seine River and take in the beautiful architecture of the surrounding buildings.

5. Visit the nearby Musée d'Orsay to see some of the world's most famous Impressionist paintings.
