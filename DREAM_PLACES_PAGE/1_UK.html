WHAT AND WHERE IS BIG BEN?

          Big Ben is a clock tower located in the Palace of Westminster in London, England. It is known for its distinctive chimes and has become an iconic symbol of the city and the United Kingdom.

WHY DO I WANT TO VISIT THIS PLACE?

          Big Ben is an architectural masterpiece and an iconic symbol of history, culture, and tradition. I want to visit this famous clock tower to witness its grandeur, marvel at its intricate design, and learn about its role in the cultural and political life of London and the United Kingdom.

OTHER THINGS TO DO AROUND THE AREA:

1. Take a guided tour of the Houses of Parliament, which is adjacent to Big Ben.

2. Stroll along the South Bank of the Thames, which offers stunning views of the clock tower and the cityscape.

3. Visit Westminster Abbey, a historic church where many famous Britons are buried.

4. Watch the Changing of the Guard at nearby Buckingham Palace.

5. Explore the Churchill War Rooms, an underground complex used during World War II.
