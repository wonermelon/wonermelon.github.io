Verses of my Soul: An Oeuvre of Poetry Writings

I've always had excessive intrusive thoughts for as long as I can remember, and it's been a huge struggle for me growing up because I wasn't raised in a family where you could freely share your thoughts and feelings. That's what inspired me to write. I became interested in poetry writing when I was 10 years old, and it has since become a coping mechanism for me whenever my mind becomes foggy. And on this page, I'm going to share some of my writings.
